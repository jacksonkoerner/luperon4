Our Process

HURRICANE PREPARATION PROCEDURES

When a storm approaches, our team follows a comprehensive preparation protocol developed over years of experience protecting vessels in Luperon Bay.

Pre-Season Preparation

Before hurricane season begins, we conduct thorough inspections of all tie-downs, anchors, and support equipment.

Storm Tracking

We monitor weather systems continuously using professional forecasting services and NOAA updates.

Pre-Storm Check

As storms approach, every vessel receives a detailed inspection and tie-down verification.

During the Storm

Our team shelters locally and conducts monitoring rounds as conditions safely allow.

Post-Storm Inspection

Immediately after conditions clear, we conduct comprehensive assessments of all stored vessels.

Owner Communication

Owners receive updates before, during (when possible), and after any significant weather event.

Storm Protocol

PREPARATION TIMELINE

5-7 Days Out

Storm tracking begins. Initial assessment of potential impact. Preliminary notifications sent to vessel owners.

3-4 Days Out

Tie-downs inspected and tightened. Loose items secured or removed. Additional straps added if needed.

24-48 Hours Out

Final preparations completed. All vessels verified secure. Team prepares for monitoring duty.

Post-Storm

As soon as conditions allow, comprehensive inspection begins. Owners notified with status reports and photos.
